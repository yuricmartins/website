Optimize

Brand creation, mobile interface, web design

THE CHALLENGE

Optimize was created by the needs of a franchise owner to understand better what was happening inside his store. He saw it as an opportunity to create a new system that could be applied to others franchises. We then developed the brand, the app and the web system.

THE SOLUTION

We create a tablet app that would be used by all the employees inside the store. They would input data like inventory count, new merchandise, internal consumption, losses and others.

As the data comes in, they are treated and then sent to a dashboard in the web system, so that the owner could control all the operation in his store. He would be able to check which day is the busiest, how much food he’s loosing in the end of the day and other information provided in the system.
