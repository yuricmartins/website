Collaris

Web design

THE CHALLENGE

Collaris is a website developed during a 36 hours Hackaton with the subject being “City security"

THE SOLUTION

We create a website where people would be able to check the safest and most dangerous areas in their cities. Gathering crime data from external websites and twitter, Collaris show them as spots on the map. To help understanding what each spot means, we developed a color and size grading for the crimes. Crimes that happened between 1 day and 1 week, are shown as big dark red spots. The spots go until light yellow, that represents crimes between 3 months and a year.

After that, crimes do not appear on the map anymore. Users can use the timeline on the bottom of the screen to watch how crimes are evolving. If the city is getting better or the crimes are relocating between neighbourhoods.
